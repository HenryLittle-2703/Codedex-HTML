About

Who am I?

Hello, my name is Gravy and I am a Leopard Gecko. I am 5 years old, which is the equivalent of 27 human years. My favorite hobbies are climbing and exploring. Other things I enjoy to do are basking at a cool 94°, eating wax worms, and napping in my human's blanket.



Stories

What do I like to eat?

I enjoy eating all kinds of insects since I am an insectivore, but I only eat every other day when I get hungry. My favorite bugs are crickets, dubia roaches, and mealworms. The ultimate best are wax worms, but I only eat those as a treat because they can make me chunky.


What are my hobbies?

I love exploring and climbing around my enclosure every day, especially at dawn and dusk. With a lot of practice, I can reach the tallest peaks of driftwood and scout the deepest corners around rocks.
